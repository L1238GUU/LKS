Krishna the lord

Krishhna is the major diety of Hinduism.

He is worshipped as the eighth avatyar of lord vishnu and also as the supreme god of his own right.He is the god of protection,compassion,tenderness andlove and is one of the most popular and widely revered among the hindu dieties.
